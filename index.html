Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game "Number To Get".

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Win the game by matching your total score to the number to get.

You lose the game if your total score goes above the number to get.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal and the number to get.
